Xirui's portfolio

Safely

    A mobile app design aimed to improve communication of accurate and detailed information about PPE usage between healthcare workers and self-isolated people, especially between those without adequate knowledge about the novel situation and how to properly protect themselves.

Uber Pool friends

    A redesign of the Uber Pool feature, by allowing users to invite a friend to their shared ride, we aim to increase time-efficiency for group rides and promote interaction between riders.

UC San Diego Library

    Collaborating with UCSD Library to oversee research on help channel access and preference across physical and virtual space. The Fi@UCSD team delivered a UX audit and redesign to the Library staff.

Pepper Canyon Designathon: UC Socially Dynamic

    With the new light rail trolley station coming to the UCSD campus, we want our students as well as visitors to feel welcomed and equipped with tools to interact with the space. Our group was one of the 5 winners with an award of $1,000.

Pet-centered design Research

    Maintaining pet health while saving space for student pet owners is a challenge. Students have hectic schedules and limited spaces in their apartments. How can we help maintain pet health and comfort with our furniture design for both pets and their humans?

P91X: Workout management App

    Gym users need a way to get in and out of the gym as quickly as possible because without it they would succumb to distractions, thus being inefficient. Our app P91X solves this problem by creating a management timer that tracks the users progress in the gym.

Neurosketch: Brain-computer interface art

    Though art may be created through mediums such as those in painting and sculpture which require gesturalization this interface design seeks to extend upon art as performance through the recording of EEG signal.

Craigslist Redesign

    A redesign of the Craigslist website after trials of user interview. The redesign was done based on errors and user needs found during user interviews. The new design will help users to find what they need more quickly and make less errors.
